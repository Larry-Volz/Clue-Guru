Free Hint On Combination Locks

Spin the dial at least three times to the right (clockwise), all the way around. This "clears" the lock of any previous numbers. If at any point in putting in your combination you mess up, do this again to start over. Stop at your first number.
For your second number, turn the dial to the left, going past zero and your first number. Then stop at your second number.
For your third number, turn the dial to the right and go directly to the last number. Leave your lock on this number. Always remember: right, left, right.
If you are pretty sure you have the combination right and can't get it open feel free to ask your game master for help. It doesn't count as a hint if it's a mechanical challenge.
